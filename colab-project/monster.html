Monsters

There are a lot of Monsters in Minecraft which are called Mobs. They come in the night and can appear in caves but they die instantly in the daytime. In some occasions some mobs can appear in the daytime for example spiders can go in the day and night but can be more aggressive in the night or if you attack them.Also Sometimes Skeletons and Zombies can stay in the day time if they wear armor but they die instantly if they are not wearing any armor.

Here are some of the mobs that are in Minecraft

  • Zombie and Drowned Zombies
  • Creeper
  • Skeleton
  • Spider
  • Witches
