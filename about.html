About

Hello, my name is McKenna!

Currently, I am a student attending Highschool, and taking classes like Computer Aided Design, Web Design, as well as multiple visual art classes. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family and pets, or practicing one of my hobbies; reading, inline skating, and art!
Based entirely on creating, problem solving, and curiosity, my life-long interest in art has lead me to pursue more design-oriented courses. Although when able I still find time to experiment with new art mediums and ultimately have fun with it.
